## Maintaining Oral Health Between Visits

Daily habits make a major difference between checkups. Brush and floss consistently, follow the 2-2-2 rule, and ask your local dentist which products best support your oral hygiene needs.

Food and lifestyle choices also affect your teeth and gums. Limiting sugary drinks, staying hydrated, and avoiding tobacco can reduce the risk of decay, staining, and gum problems.

Regular checkups remain essential even with good home care. Ongoing preventive care helps catch issues early and may reduce the need for more involved treatment later, especially for families throughout [Linden, NJ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/k1vHiQzefDGzn6ju5).

### What is the 2 2 2 rule for teeth?

The 2-2-2 rule means brushing for 2 minutes, 2 times a day, and seeing a dentist 2 times a year. It is a simple way to support preventive care and better oral hygiene.

### Can a dentist glue a crown back on?

In many cases, a dentist can recement a crown if the crown and tooth are still in good condition. Do not use household glue, and schedule an exam promptly to avoid further damage.
